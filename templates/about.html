About CareerGuide
What is CareerGuide?

CareerGuide is an intelligent, user-friendly platform designed to help students discover their ideal career paths. By leveraging advanced machine learning models and a rich dataset, CareerGuide provides personalized career suggestions based on your interests, strengths, and academic performance.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students and young professionals to make informed decisions about their future. Our platform bridges the gap between aspirations and opportunities, making career planning accessible and insightful for everyone.

How it Works
  • Fill out a simple form with your interests, strengths, and academic details.
  • Our ML model analyzes your input and suggests the most suitable career path for you.
  • Get instant, actionable feedback and explore your options!
Meet the Team

This project was created by a passionate team of students and developers for the Smart India Hackathon 2025. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and are committed to making career guidance smarter and more accessible.
